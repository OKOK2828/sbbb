Bully

SOLarship Edition

Bully is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar Vancouver and published by Rockstar Games, released on October 17, 2006 for the PlayStation 2. A remastered version of the game, subtitled Scholarship Edition, was developed by Mad Doc Software and released for Xbox 360 and Wii on March 4, 2008 and for Microsoft Windows on October 21 of the same year. Bully was re-released for PlayStation 4 via PlayStation Network on March 22, 2016.
